11. What is apiculture? What are the requirements to consider for bee–keeping?
Ans
.The culturing of honey bees for the production of honey or beewax is called Apiculture.
Bee – keeping can be practised in any area where there is sufficient bee pastures of some
wild shrubs, fruits orchards & cultivated crops. The following points are important for
successful bee – keeping :-
1. Knowledge of nature & habits of bee.
2. Selection of suitable location of keeping beehives.
3. Catching & hiving of swarms.
4. Management of beehives during different seasons.
5. Handling & collection of honey &beewax.
