12. A sperm has just fertilized a human egg in the fallopian tube. Trace the events that
the fertilized eggs will undergoes upto implantation of blastocyst in the uterus.
Ans.
1. CLEAVAGE :-Fertilized egg starts dividing lay specific mitotic divisions called
cleavage. The zygotes undergoes mitotic division in the isthmus of oviduct to form daughter
cell the cells formed as a result of cleavage called blastomere
2. BLASTOCYST :- 3-4 days after fertilization, the morula twins into large mass of cells called
blastocyst Outer peripheral cells enlarge & flatten further & form trophoblast. Trophoblast
cells secretes a fluid into interior & form a cavity called blastocoel. The embryonic stage with
blastocoels is called blastula.
