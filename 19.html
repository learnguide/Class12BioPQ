22. Why is process of fertilization in flowering plants referred to as double
fertilization?
Ans.
In flowering plants, the first male nuclei fuses with egg to form a diploid zygote &
Second male nuclei fuses with Secondary nucleus to give rise to primary endosperm nucleus
– thus process of fertilization twice in an embryo sac. & therefore called DOUBLE
FERTILISATION.
23.What are cleistogamous flowers? Can cross – pollination occurs in cleistogamous
flowers. Give reason?
Ans.
In some Angiospermic plants eg. Commelina, Oxalis etc, flowers are bisexual & they
never open. This condition is called cleistogamy & flowers are called cleistogamous
cleistogamous flowers are self – Pollination & to ensure this they never open Hence, cross
pollination is not possible.
