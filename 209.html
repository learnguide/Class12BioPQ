12.An orchid plant is growing on the branch of mango tree. How do you describe this
interaction between the orchid & the mango tree?
Ans.
Orchids grows as epiphytes on mango tree. This is an example of commensalism in
which orchids are benefited by getting a shelter while the tree is neither benefited nor
harmed.
13.State Gauss’s competitive exclusion principle?
Ans.
Gause’s competitive exclusion principle states that two closely related species competing
for the same resources cannot exist together as the competitively inferior one will be
eliminated but this is true only when resources are limiting & not otherwise.
