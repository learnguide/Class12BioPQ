GERMINATION OF POLLEN GRAINS ON STIGMA
The pollen grains absorb fluid present on stigma & swell up. The exine ruptures at the place
of germ pore & intine comes out in the form of tube with its internal contents. This small
tubular structure is called pollen tube & process is called pollen germination.
