16. Why do most zygotes develop after certain amount of embryo is formed?
Ans.
The zygote divides only after certain amount of endosperm is formed as it is an
adaptation to provide assured nutrition to the developing embryo.
17. What is polyembryony?
Ans.
Polyembryony is the phenomenon of formation of more than one embryo during the
development of seed.
