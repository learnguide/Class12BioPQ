8.What is “tissue culture”. What are the steps involved in tissue culture?
Ans
.“Tissue culture is an experimental process through which a mass of cells (callus) is
produced froman explant tissue & used directly to regenerate plant It invoices following
steps :-
1. Selection of an elite plant
2. Preparation of suitable culture media
3. Sterilisation of an explant & inoculation on culture media under controlled temp ~ 250 c
in light
4. Callus induction in explant.
5. Organogenesis :- a high cytokine : auxin ratio induce Shoot formation while high auxin :
cytokinin ratio induce root formation.
6. Acclamatization :- test tube rooted plantlets are first subjected to acclimatization in green
house & then transferred to the field.
