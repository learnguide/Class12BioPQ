3. Why are proteins synthesized from Spirulina called Single celled Proteins? What is
the significance of such a protein?
Ans.
The protein rich food produced by microbes is called as single called protein (SCP)
Spirulina is a microorganisms which has more protein. It is a quick method of protein
production because the growth rate of microbes is enormous. Hence, it provides a protein
rich diet for human beings.
