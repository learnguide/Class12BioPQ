4.What is IUCN red list? Give any two uses of this list?
Ans
. IUCN (International union of conservation of nature & natural resources) maintains a
“Red datalist” which is a catalogue of taxa facing risk of extinction. The main purpose of this
list:-
i) to identify & document the species with high risk of extinction.
ii) to provide awareness to the degree of threat to biodiversity.
5.“Species diversity of plants is much less than that of animals” Why?
Ans
.The species diversity of plants is much less than that of animals because most animals
