24.Mention any two ways in which organisms tide over unfavourable conditions by
suspending their activities.
Ans. i)
Hibernation – phenomenon of spending cold period in inactive stage by an animal
e.g. frog,reptiles, polar bear.
ii)
Aestivation – phenomenon of spending dry & hot conditions in an inactive stage by an
animal e.g. snail, fishes.
