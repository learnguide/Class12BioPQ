8.Distinguish between ectotherms& Endotherms?
Ans.
Ectotherms are those animals whose body temperature changes & matches with that of
environment in which they are living whereas Endotherms are those animal whose body
temperature is maintained relatively constant by physiological regulation.
9.“Lichens are considered good examples of obligate mutualisms”. Comment?
Ans.
Lichens show an intimate mutualistic relationship between a fungus & an algae or
cynobacterium where the fungus helps in absorption of nutrients & provides it to bacteria
while the algae or cyanobacterium prepares the food.
