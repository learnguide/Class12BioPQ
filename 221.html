there is no net increase in the number of cells.

5.Give an example to show that completely unrelated species can also compete for
same resources?
Ans.
Completely unrelated species can also compete for same resources for e.g. In certain
shallow lakes of South America the visiting flamingoes & the native fishes compete for the
same zooplanktons as their food.
