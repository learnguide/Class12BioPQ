5. State the 4 criteria which a molecule must fulfill to act as a genetic material.
Ans.(i)
It should be able to generate its replica.
(ii)
Should be chemically and structurally stable.
(iii)
Should be able to express itself in the form of Mendelian characters.
(iv)
Should provide the scope for slow changes (mutations) that are necessary for evolution.
