2 Marks Questions
1. In angiospermic plant before formation of microspore sporogenous tissue undergo
cell division
(a) Name the type of cell division.
(b) What would be the ploidy of the cells of tetrad?
Ans.
(a) meiosis division (b) haploid
2. Outer envelop of pollen grain made of a highly resistant substance. What is that
substance? At which particular point the substance is not present?
Ans.
Sporopollenin; at germpore sporopollenin is absent.
3. Fruits generally develops from ovary, but in few species thalamus contributes to
fruit formation.
(a) Name the two categories of fruits.
(b) Give one example of each.
Ans.
Two categories of fruits are
(i) True fruits e.g., Mango
(ii) False fruit e.g., Apple
4. Among the animal, insects particularly bees are the dominant pollinating agents. List
any four characteristic features of the insect pollinated flower.
Ans. i
. Flowers are large.
ii. Colorful petals of flower.
iii. Presence of fragrance.
iv. Rich in nectar.
