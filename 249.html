9.What do you mean lay species diversity? Name two measures of species diversity?
Ans
.Species diversity refers to the variety of species within a region. The two important
measuresOf specie diversity are:-
i) Species richness:- It refers to number of species per unit area.
ii) Species evenness :- It refers to relative abundance with which each species is represented
in an area.
