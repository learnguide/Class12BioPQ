9. What are the limitations of ecological pyramids?
Ans
.
(i)
Does not take into account same species belonging to two or more trophic levels.
(ii)
Assumes simple food chain, does not accomodate food web.
(iii)
Saprophytes have not been given any place in ecological pyramids.
