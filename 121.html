15.What are the three different ways in which selection may occur.
Ans.
The three different ways in which selection may occur are as below:-
(i)
StablisingSelection :- Individuals with intermediate value of heritable phenotypic
characteristics are favoured over other individuals.
(ii)
Directional Selection :- Individuals with one extreme of heritable phenotypic
characteristic have an advantage over individuals in a population.
