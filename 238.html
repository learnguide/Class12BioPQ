27.Differentiate between primary productivity & secondary productivity?
Ans
.Primary productivity refers to productivity at trophic level i.e. food energy formed by
way of photosynthesis using solar energy whereas secondary productivity refers to gross
productivity minus losses by way of respiration & decomposition.
